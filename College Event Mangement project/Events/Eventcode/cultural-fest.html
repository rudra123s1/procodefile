About Aura 2025

Experience the magic of 'Aura', our annual cultural festival! Aura is a vibrant mosaic of India's rich heritage, featuring mesmerizing music concerts, breathtaking dance performances, captivating dramas, and exquisite art exhibitions. It's a platform for students to express their creativity and a spectacle for all to enjoy. Join us for a day filled with color, energy, and unforgettable memories.
